Free Time?

Music and Reading

I have a passion for both music and non-fiction literature, particularly in the realms of physics and anthropology. My curiosity knows no bounds, and anthropology appeals to my interest in the world's history, including my fascination with dinosaurs and fossils.

For a tranquil study atmosphere, I turn to classical music and find solace in piano covers of anime soundtracks. When the mood for non-fiction takes a backseat, I immerse myself in the captivating worlds of manga and light novels, allowing my imagination to run wild. As for my favorite dinosaur, it's hard to pick just one, but the vicious Pterodactyl has always held a special place in my heart. Feel free to share your favorite dinosaur too!
